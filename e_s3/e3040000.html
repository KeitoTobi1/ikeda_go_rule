3.4 The Two Types of Concise and Complete Area Rules

Formulations of the two types of area rules described so far are given in Part I, together with a third type of area rules and three types of territory rules.
Area rules I and II differ on whether there is a cutting bonus, and on whether points like 'a' and 'b' in Dia. 3-3-4 are counted. The situation in Dia. 3-3-4 is that White has no possible move anywhere. The chance that such a situation will ever occur in actual play is vanishingly small, so for all practical purposes, the only difference between these two area rules is the cutting bonus.*
